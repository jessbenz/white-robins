Company Mission

At White Robins, our mission is to provide a safe and secure online service that enables you to take a positive and proactive approach to your end of life plan.

Our service empowers you to become part of the healing process for your loved ones after you have passed by creating a legacy that can be shared for generations to come.

About Us

Wendy Coyle is a past honours degree business graduate from the Institute of Technology, Blanchardstown. In the final years of her study she was faced with the loss of her father-in-law Liam, shortly after her mum Joanne was also diagnosed with stage 4 cancer and her father James suffered a brain aneurism which left him paralysed and with long-term brain damage. Through these experiences she was driven by a desire to capture memories of her parents to share with her siblings as keepsakes for the future.

Realising the benefits of these messages, she too started to record messages for her children Taylor-Jade and Brandon to help them through their grief for a day when she would no longer be there to guide them through their life journey. With a passionate belief in the value the White Robins service can provide to families and friends after a person has passed, she decided to continue her studies. In 2016 she received a post-graduate in Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Enterprise from the Innovation Academy in the University College Dublin.

Today, she works full-time developing the business and is proud to introduce the White Robins services to the market in early 2018.
